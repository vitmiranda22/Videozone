Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Video_maker is a personal, single-user content creation tool. This policy explains what data it accesses and how it is used.

What data is accessed

When you connect a social media account (YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram) to this application, it requests OAuth access tokens that allow it to publish video content on your behalf to that account.

How data is used

Access tokens and account data are used exclusively to generate and publish video content to the connected accounts. No data is sold, shared, or transmitted to any third party.

Where data is stored

All access tokens and account data are stored locally on the developer's own device. Nothing is stored on a remote server operated by this application.

Data retention and deletion

You (the account owner) can revoke access at any time by disconnecting the account within the application, or by removing the app's access directly from the connected platform's account settings. Disconnecting deletes the locally stored token immediately.
